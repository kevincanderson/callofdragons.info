alistair

cavalry | rally | tank

An ever-reliable knight of the Royal Court, Alistair's melancholy eyes hide a long past.

Alistair is a powerful and honorable cavalry hero in Call of Dragons, known for his strong leadership and frontline prowess. As a noble knight of the League of Order, Alistair embodies valor and discipline, making him a reliable choice for players who favor mounted combat and aggressive tactics. His skills focus on boosting cavalry unit strength, enhancing their damage and mobility, and offering sustainability in prolonged battles. Whether leading charges against enemy forces or defending strategic locations, Alistair excels in both PvE and PvP scenarios. His presence on the battlefield brings a commanding advantage, especially when paired with other cavalry-focused heroes.
